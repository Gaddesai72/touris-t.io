Tourist Places in Tirumala

Tirumala Venkateswara Temple

The most famous temple dedicated to Lord Venkateswara, it is one of the richest and most visited religious sites in the world.

Sri Vari Padalu

Located on Narayanagiri Hill, Sri Vari Padalu is believed to be the place where Lord Venkateswara first set foot on Tirumala.

Silathoranam

A natural rock formation that resembles an arch, Silathoranam is considered a geological wonder and a sacred site.

Akasa Ganga

A natural waterfall near Tirumala, Akasa Ganga holds religious significance and is a beautiful spot for nature lovers.

Papavinasanam

A holy waterfall where pilgrims believe that taking a dip can wash away their sins. The location offers scenic views.

Chakra Theertham

A sacred water body believed to be created by the Sudarshana Chakra of Lord Vishnu. It's a serene and spiritual spot.

Swami Pushkarini Lake

A sacred lake near the temple where devotees take a ritual bath before entering the temple.

Japali Teertham

A serene temple dedicated to Lord Hanuman, located in a forested area. A great place for meditation and prayer.

Venugopala Swamy Temple

A temple dedicated to Lord Krishna, offering a peaceful and less crowded spiritual experience.

Srivari Museum

The Srivari Museum showcases the history, architecture, and traditions of Tirumala Temple.

Rock Garden (Shila Thoranam Garden)

A beautifully landscaped garden near Silathoranam with scenic views and interesting rock formations.
