Tourist Places in Vizag

RK Beach

Ramakrishna Beach (RK Beach) is a popular spot in Vizag, known for its beautiful coastline, serene atmosphere, and various attractions like the INS Kursura Submarine Museum.

Kailasagiri

Kailasagiri is a scenic hilltop park offering panoramic views of the city and the coastline. The giant statues of Lord Shiva and Parvati are a major attraction.

Araku Valley

Araku Valley is a picturesque hill station near Vizag, known for its lush greenery, coffee plantations, and tribal culture. The journey to Araku through tunnels and bridges is an experience in itself.

Borra Caves

Borra Caves are natural limestone caves located near Araku Valley, famous for their stunning stalactites and stalagmites. These caves are a must-visit for nature and adventure enthusiasts.

Simhachalam Temple

Simhachalam Temple is an ancient temple dedicated to Lord Narasimha, located on a hilltop. The temple's unique architecture and religious significance make it a prominent pilgrimage site.

Yarada Beach

Yarada Beach is a secluded and serene beach located near Vizag, offering pristine sands and tranquil waters. It’s an ideal spot for relaxation away from the crowds.

Indira Gandhi Zoological Park

Indira Gandhi Zoological Park is a large zoo located amidst the Eastern Ghats, home to a wide variety of animals, birds, and reptiles. It’s a great place for a family outing.

Rushikonda Beach

Rushikonda Beach is a well-known beach in Vizag, popular for its golden sands, clear waters, and water sports activities. It’s a favorite destination for both locals and tourists.

Dolphin's Nose

Dolphin's Nose is a natural rock formation resembling a dolphin's nose, offering breathtaking views of the Bay of Bengal and the surrounding landscape. The lighthouse here is also a popular attraction.

Thotlakonda

Thotlakonda is an ancient Buddhist site located on a hill near Vizag, featuring stupas, viharas, and other remnants of Buddhist culture. It’s a peaceful spot with historical significance.
