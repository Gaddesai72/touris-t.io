Tourist Places in Godavari

Papikondalu

Papikondalu is a scenic hill range located along the banks of the Godavari River. Known for its lush green hills, deep valleys, and serene river, it offers a perfect escape into nature with stunning boat rides and tribal culture.

Rajahmundry

Rajahmundry is a historic city on the banks of the Godavari River, known for its cultural significance, picturesque landscapes, ancient temples, and vibrant arts scene. The Godavari Bridge is a major attraction here.

Draksharamam

Draksharamam is an ancient temple town known for the Bhimeswara Swamy Temple, a significant pilgrimage site and one of the Pancharama Kshetras. The temple's architecture is a blend of Chalukyan and Chola styles.

Dindi

Dindi is a tranquil village surrounded by lush green coconut groves, offering peaceful backwaters and traditional cuisine. A perfect destination for a relaxing getaway in the Konaseema region.

Kolleru Lake

Kolleru Lake is one of the largest freshwater lakes in India, known for its rich biodiversity and bird-watching opportunities. The lake is a designated wildlife sanctuary, offering a haven for migratory birds.

Antarvedi

Antarvedi is a coastal village known for the Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy Temple and the confluence of the Godavari River with the Bay of Bengal. A spiritually significant and scenic destination.

Maredumilli

Maredumilli is a picturesque hill station known for its dense forests, waterfalls, and tribal culture. It's an ideal spot for trekking, nature exploration, and experiencing the traditional lifestyle of indigenous communities.

Coringa Wildlife Sanctuary

Coringa Wildlife Sanctuary is a large mangrove forest near Kakinada, offering rich biodiversity, especially for bird watchers. The sanctuary is a peaceful escape into nature, with boat rides and scenic views.

Kadiyapulanka

Kadiyapulanka is a village famous for its flower nurseries and horticulture. It's a peaceful and picturesque destination, ideal for those interested in gardening and nature.

Kakinada Beach

Kakinada Beach offers a blend of relaxation and recreation with clean sands, calm waters, and various activities. It's a popular destination for families and tourists, known for its serene environment.
